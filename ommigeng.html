About Me

My name is Bobin Lilja, a systems administrator and Microsoft 365 specialist passionate about inclusive IT solutions. Since 2016, I have worked with support, operations, and cloud services—always focusing on the user experience. I grew up in a challenging environment where few believed in my ambitions, but through persistence and hard work, I proved you can go far.

Throughout my career, I have implemented Intune solutions, configured Power Automate flows, and built internal service functions from scratch. I have experience leading projects in cross-functional teams and know how to create clear processes to maintain high quality.

As a transgender woman, I understand the value of representation and actively work to increase inclusion in the tech industry. I live openly as pansexual and polyamorous, with honesty as the foundation of all my relationships. My ADHD has taught me to optimize workflows, and I use digital tools to structure both work and leisure time.

In my free time, I enjoy single-player gaming, testing corporate IT systems, and creating outfits. I also love long road trips in my car while listening to audiobooks or my favorite music. My signature hat, along with my unique style, makes a lasting impression.

I have previously been a volunteer with Stockholm Pride, where I have coordinated security and dispatch teams for several years.

As a speaker, I enjoy discussing LGBTQ+ rights, diversity, and the role of technology in societal development, and I have also given internal company talks on inclusion.

My work philosophy is built on continuous improvement, open collaboration, and active listening. I believe in building bridges between IT and other disciplines like HR to create holistic solutions, and I am interested in social and political issues so I can contribute to positive change.

I have experience developing user support policies and documenting processes for long-term stability. I run a TikTok channel where I talk about being trans and inclusion, and I share insights on LinkedIn. I enjoy contributing to internal networks and discussion groups to share experiences and learn from others.

I teach workshops on Microsoft 365, technology, and inclusion for colleagues and end users. I continually invest in my skills through courses and home lab experiments.

Privately, I cherish quiet moments at home with family or a good conversation over coffee. My partner and I are currently exploring Gothenburg and discovering new favorite spots together.

I am proud to have spoken at conferences about technology, diversity, and my personal journey. My vision for the future is to lead projects that combine technology, sustainability, and inclusion in society.

If you'd like to discuss collaborations, ideas, or just chat about IT and life—get in touch for a coffee and conversation!
